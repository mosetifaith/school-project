Beef Masala

Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Spices

Instructions:

  1. Chop the onions into smaller pieces and fry then in three table spoons of oil, let them turn golden brown and then add the chopped meat.
  2. Chop the tomatoes into smaller pieces, and the blend them to get a thick paste, let it reast white the mmeat shallow fries in the onions till it is ready for the paste.
  3. Add the paste to the meat, stir it gently while you add the spices; royco, Curry powder and the garam masala as well as the salt. let it cook on medium heat.
  4. After making sure the meat is ready, take it out and serve with rice or any meal of your choice.
